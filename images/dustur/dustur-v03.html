Düstur 3 of Tertib-i Evvel. Der-i Saʿadet: Maṭbaʿa-yi ʿĀmire, 1289 aH [1872/3].

This demo website is meant to illustrate the issue of available copies of Düstur differing substantially in layout, pagination, spelling, and content. I assigned the three provided copies to two editions, based on the substantial differences in both layout and content. I have further subdivided the second edition into two print-runs, which differ in spelling and printing errors (for the lack of a better term)

Edition 1

Held by Hakkı Tarık Us Collection and School of Oriental and African Studies
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Edition 2

Held by University of California, Berkeley. This edition uses different spelling. Marginally different page layout.
